SciFi franchises:

Coaxium (found on an outer planet's moon. Star Wars) - useful for faster space travel.

Naquadah (very rare, Stargate) - useful for wormhole travel.

Adamantium (very rare metal, Marvel) - useful for better weaponry and armor.

SciFi in general:

Tachyon (warpspeed, not time travel. very very difficult to capture enough early-FTL, easier as time goes on) - useful for FTL drives. /p>

Map-specific (can have modded materials):

Cryotheum - coldest material.

Pyromethium - hottest non-plasma material.

Lore-specific:

Hybrid Energy - Cosmosian gas (available to all eventually, gives a major buff to anything infused with it. rare in the beginning, uncommon later)

Derezqueium - Novayanan Material (late-game material similar to tachyons)

Derzite - Novayanan Material (rare earth metal, midpoint between Titanium and Adamantium, component for making Derezqueium later on)

Cosmosium - Cosmosian element (only found in cosmosia unless Space says someone else can have some)
